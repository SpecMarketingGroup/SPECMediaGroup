Our Story

A Philadelphia Brand
Built From The Ground Up

SPEC Media Group was founded by E. Gabriel Lloyd, a Magna Cum Laude Business Marketing graduate from West Chester University with over five years of hands-on experience shooting, editing, and marketing for businesses across the Greater Philadelphia Area.

What started as a passion for photography and visual storytelling grew into a full-service creative and digital marketing company. Every project we take on is treated like it's our own brand on the line — because we know what it feels like to build something and need it to be represented the right way.

We work exclusively with businesses. That focus is intentional. B2B clients have different needs, different timelines, and different expectations than personal projects — and we've built SPEC around delivering at that level every time.

From a restaurant in Delaware County to a real estate agent in the Main Line, every client gets the same level of professionalism, preparation, and care that we'd want for our own brand.
