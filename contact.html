Our Story

A Studio That Actually Cares

SPEC Media Group is a boutique creative studio serving small businesses across Delaware and Chester County. Founded by Gabriel Lloyd, a Magna Cum Laude Business Marketing graduate from West Chester University with over five years of hands-on experience shooting, editing, and marketing for businesses across the Greater Philadelphia Area.

Through SPEC Media, we produce photo and video content that elevates how brands show up online, content that stops the scroll, excites customers, and reflects the quality of what the business actually does.

Through SPEC Marketing, we go beyond execution, collaborating with clients to develop ideas, build strategy, and manage content that drives engagement and brings real traffic to their business.

We're not just a production shop. We're a hands-on creative partner invested in what happens after the camera stops rolling.
