Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before booking with SPEC Media Group.

How do I book a shoot?

Fill out the contact form above or call/text us directly. We'll confirm availability, send you a quote, and secure your date with a 50% non-refundable deposit.

How much is the deposit?

A non-refundable 50% deposit is required to lock in your shoot date. The remaining balance is due before your final files are released.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the Greater Philadelphia Area including Delaware County, Chester County, Philadelphia, the Main Line, and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply for shoots outside the region.

How long until I get my files?

Photography: 3–5 business days. 30-second video: 5–7 business days. 60-second video: 7–10 business days. Rush options available on every service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Zelle (preferred), PayPal Business, Venmo Business, and cash for local transactions. Invoices unpaid after 7 days are subject to a 1.5% weekly late fee.

Can I reschedule my shoot?

One reschedule is permitted with at least 24 hours notice. Cancellations or reschedule requests within 24 hours may result in forfeiture of your deposit.

What do I need to prepare before the shoot?

We'll cover everything in your pre-production consultation call. Generally: have your branding materials, logos, and any specific messaging ready. For real estate, ensure the property is clean and staged.

Do you own the footage after delivery?

Upon full payment you receive usage rights for business and promotional purposes. Raw unedited footage remains SPEC Media Group's property unless separately purchased ($150–$200 flat).

How does the Content Management Add-On work?

You provide platform access and deliver your content each month. We write the captions, apply hashtags, and schedule everything to post across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn on your behalf.

Can I use my content for paid ads?

Yes — your usage rights cover business and promotional purposes including paid advertising. If you need specific ad formats or sizes, let us know at booking and we'll format accordingly.
