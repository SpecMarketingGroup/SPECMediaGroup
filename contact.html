Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I book a shoot?

Call, text, or email us directly. We'll confirm availability, send your quote, and secure your date with a 50% non-refundable deposit.

How much is the deposit?

A non-refundable 50% deposit is required to lock in your shoot date. The remaining balance is due before final files are released.

What areas do you serve?

Greater Philadelphia Area, Delaware County, Chester County, Philadelphia, the Main Line, and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply outside the region.

How long until I get my files?

Promo videos typically deliver in 5-10 business days, event coverage in 7-14. Rush delivery is available on every service, just ask.

Can I reschedule my shoot?

One reschedule is permitted with at least 24 hours notice. Cancellations or requests within 24 hours may result in forfeiture of your deposit.

Do you post to my accounts?

Your accounts always remain yours. On a retainer, SPEC manages your pages and posting remotely on your behalf. If you prefer to keep posting in-house, we deliver the content and a clear posting plan instead.

Who do I work with?

You work directly with the SPEC Media Group team from the first conversation through final delivery. No account managers, no hand-offs.
