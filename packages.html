Good To Know

Booking & Payment
Policies

Clear and straightforward — so there are no surprises when we work together.

Deposit & Payment

  • A non-refundable 50% deposit is required to secure any booking
  • Remaining balance is due before final files are released
  • Invoices unpaid after 7 days are subject to a 1.5% weekly late fee
  • Clients paying $600+ in a calendar year will receive a W-9 request

Payment Methods

  • Zelle — preferred, fast, no fees
  • PayPal Business — accepted, paper trail friendly
  • Venmo Business — accepted for existing Venmo clients
  • Cash — available for local/small transactions

Rescheduling & Cancellation

  • One reschedule permitted with at least 24 hours notice
  • Cancellations within 24 hours may result in deposit forfeiture
  • No-shows result in automatic deposit forfeiture
  • Package shoots unused after 3 months from booking expire

Deliverables & Rights

  • Final files delivered digitally after full payment received
  • Files stored for 30 days after delivery — download promptly
  • Client receives usage rights for business and promotional use
  • Raw footage remains SPEC property unless separately purchased
