The Process

How It Works

A repeatable monthly process designed to keep your content consistent and your audience growing.

01

Onboarding

We align on your brand, goals, upcoming events, specials, and anything you want to highlight that month.

02

Shoot Days

On-site shoot days at the pace your plan sets, short-form content, long-form video, photos, and graphic source material all captured on-site.

03

Post-Production

Every deliverable edited in-house: color grade, sound design, captions, retouching, and graphic overlays.

04

All content edited and finalized, then posted and managed directly on your pages by SPEC, on a schedule built for growth.

05

Repeat

Every month, fresh content. We adjust the strategy based on what's working and what your audience responds to.

Retainer Terms

What To Expect

Payment Schedule

First month's payment is due in full before work begins or any shoot is scheduled. Subsequent months are invoiced on the 1st and due by the 5th.

Cancellation Policy

Month-to-month clients require 30-day written notice to cancel. 3-month clients commit to the full 3-month term.

Ad Spend

Ad spend is billed directly by Meta to the client. SPEC manages your pages and ad campaigns remotely as part of a retainer. Full management requires account access; if you prefer not to grant access, we deliver content and a posting plan instead.

Content Ownership

All content and accounts remain the property of the client. If we ever part ways, everything stays with you.
