The Process

How It Works

A repeatable monthly process designed to keep your content consistent and your audience growing.

01

Onboarding

We align on your brand, goals, upcoming events, specials, and anything you want to highlight that month.

02

Shoot Days

Two visits to your location — short-form content, long-form video, photos, and graphic source material all captured on-site.

03

Post-Production

Every deliverable edited in-house: color grade, sound design, captions, retouching, and graphic overlays.

04

Delivery

All content delivered in a shared folder, organized and ready to post — with the Meta ad guide and posting schedule.

05

Repeat

Every month, fresh content. We adjust the strategy based on what's working and what your audience responds to.

Retainer Terms

What To Expect

Payment Schedule

First month's payment is due in full before work begins or any shoot is scheduled. Subsequent months are invoiced on the 1st and due by the 5th.

Cancellation Policy

Month-to-month clients require 30-day written notice to cancel. 3-month clients commit to the full 3-month term.

Ad Spend

Ad spend is paid directly by the client to Meta. We do not manage ad budgets or access client accounts — only content creation and the targeting guide.

Content Ownership

All content remains the property of the client upon full payment. We never access social accounts or passwords.
